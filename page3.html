Project Description

T his is a group project about a horror film where a girl tries on a necklace she orders. As time passes, strange things begin to happen. We spent a month f rom creating the script to filming and later editing the video. A fter the project was assigned, my group members and I started brainstorming about the theme and types we will do. We created an idea bank on google docs and vote for the best idea. I sugges t ed my members to use a horror theme, as I researched the past SIAT projects, I found not many teams would work on horror theme as it has high demands on the design of scenery, sound, and graphics. I t could be a challenge for us but also an opportunity. Si nce this is one of the few film classes we get to experience in SIAT, I hope d we could get out of our comfort zone and produce a triumphant piece My team members agreed, and I ended up taking on the role of primary screenwriter.
(Team size: 4 / Project Duration: 10 weeks)

Stage 1 - Script Writing

After a large amount time of researching, as well as considered about the time and technical we had, I got some inspirations from “ A Social Life ” and Ghost pass by ”. In my initial thoughts, I designed the scenario to discuss the horrors that arise from the relationship between social media and human nature like Social Media Depression. It's about a girl who loves taking selfies and posting them everywhere she goes every time she uses her phone, she saw a ghost behind and getting closer

Stage 2 - Create Storyboard

The topic get approved at fi rst and then we started to make a draft story broad and discuss about the film log we would film with . To be precise about the timeline, I created a production schedule to make sure we are on the right track on each step.

Stage 3 - Create VFX

I was also responsible for the visual effects for the credit section which is consistent with the tone of the film and part of the visual effects of the ghost face at last.

Stage 4 - Project Reflection

I think the key to our success is that we communicate very promptly and accurately. As the promoter of the whole project, I have a very strict control over the time, in time we had a huge amount of tasks, we still completed all the work. At the same time, at the end of each shoot, I would call a small meeting to summarize the day's accomplishments and areas for improvement, so our teamwork was very harmonious. During the production process, I paid great attention to the audience's thoughts and feelings, esp ecially during the final revision process, I asked a lot of friends for feedback, so that they could tell me how they feel the pace and style of the whole film and I can make adjustments on time
